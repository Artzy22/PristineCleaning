Common questions

Quick answers — and we're always a call or text away if you have more.

How fast do you get back to me after I request a quote?
A team member calls or texts within the next 5 minutes during business hours (Mon-Sat, 7am-7pm). After hours, you'll hear from us first thing the next morning.
What's included in a standard clean?
Kitchen (counters, sinks, appliances exterior, floors), bathrooms (toilets, sinks, tubs, mirrors, floors), dusting all surfaces, vacuuming, mopping, trash. Add-ons available for inside oven, inside fridge, laundry, and more — just ask.
How does pricing work?
Every quote is custom, based on your home size, frequency, and any specific needs. There's no flat rate because no two homes are the same. The 30-second quiz gives us what we need to put together an accurate quote during your callback.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Up to you. Most of our recurring clients give us a key, code, or garage entry so they can come home to a fresh house. Every team member is vetted and trusted — we treat your home like our own.
What if I'm not happy with the clean?
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If anything isn't perfect, just let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back and re-clean it, at no charge. Period.
Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes — we bring everything. Eco-friendly, pet-safe products by default. If you have specific products you'd like us to use instead (or skip), just let us know.
What areas do you serve?
We service the North Atlanta metro — Marietta, Lawrenceville, Norcross, Roswell, Sandy Springs, Alpharetta, Smyrna, Dunwoody, Tucker, and surrounding areas. Not sure if you're in our zone? Mention your zip in the quiz and we'll confirm on the call.
How do I cancel or reschedule?
No long-term commitment, no cancellation fees. Just give us 24 hours notice and we'll handle it. You're always in control of your schedule.
